Who is an Agent Institution?


Agent institutions are the entities who are currently in the bill payment, collection and aggregation business.
They have not got in-principal approval from rbi or have not applied for the same.
These are the entities that could be on-boarded in the bbps ecosystem by entities called as bbpous.
Agent-institutions may further on-board agents and/or set-up customer service points in various regions and locations.


Benefits of becoming an Agent Institution


Agent-institution can become the easiest touch-points for customer

Easier integration with a consumer bbpou, who has got in-principal approval from rbi

Can get the pie of off-us transactions and consumer can pay for any biller in the bbps ecosystem even without integrating with billers and aggregators

Increased revenue opportunities due to increase in bill payment transactions of various biller categories
