BBPS... the better way to manage bills!

The first of its kind bill payment system in India is here to change the way bills are paid. Bringing a revolution in India's bill payment market, BBPS enables every citizen to pay their different bills under the same window. This unified platform brings banks, ecommerce portals and online payment platforms under one roof as its official operational units for bill collections.

BBPS PARTNERS
Easy for you and your customers!

Once you get listed as an authorized BBPS partner, your customers will enjoy the ease of hassle-free bill payments. Also, it's a great opportunity for you to enhance your customer's trust. BBPS allows you to build healthy customer relationships, and leverages your business network.

BILLERS
Enjoy easier integration!

No more chasing after bills. With BBPS, you can collect your bills fast and keep track of all payments. It offers a wider reach through different channels of payment and helps you earn your customer's trust.

CUSTOMERS
Managing bills get better with BBPS!

Wherever you go, your bills will follow you. Pay anywhere, anytime across the country with great convenience and secure payments. No more queues or hassles of managing every bill separately. It's you who decide how, when and where to pay.
