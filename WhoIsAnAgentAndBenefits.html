Who is an Agent?

BECOME THE EASIEST TOUCHPOINT FOR A CUSTOMER
Agents are the customer touch points and service points in the BBPS Ecosystem which will be available in the following forms:

  • - Branch Offices
  • - Collection centres
  • - Retail Outlets
  • - Other Services Points

An Agent would be sole the touch-point for Customers to walk in any outlet/service point with BBPS Logo and make a bill payment.

Agents can accept bill payments through various modes. Agents may be on-boarded either directly by BBPOU or by Agent Institutions to offer BBPS services.

Benefits of becoming an Agent
Increased revenue opportunities due to increase in bill payment transactions

No major need to expand the existing infrastructure
Increased customer footfall and hence cross selling opportunities

Increased customer trust due to associations with BBPS

Roles & Responsiblities
Customer Touch-point

Shall provide the customer touch points for payment of bills issued by any biller who is a participant in the BBPS will be accepted.

Customer Assistance

Facilitate BBPS registration by customers who opt to do so and also assist the registered customers to map billers to their registered id, as desired by the customer.

BBPS Brand Visibility

A BBPS logo or trademark will be prominently displayed at the collection points as per the guidelines laid down by BBPCU to identify agent's outlet as BBPS enabled Service Point.

Compliance

Compliance and adherence to all procedural guidelines and standards of BBPS.

Handling Complaints & Disputes

Agents to assist customers for grievance and other transactional complaints. Also, sensitively handle the customer in case of any dispute and grievance/s.

Instant Receipt and Confirmation

The customer shall be given an instant confirmation of bill payments and printed receipt in accordance with the standards and guidelines of BBPS.

Process of becoming an Agent

What is the On-boarding Process and Pre-requisites?

1
Agents will submit a BBPS participation letter to BBPOU mentioning name of core BBPOU as well.
2
Application for accessing BBPS Application module from BBPOU.
3
BBPOU shall confirm the availability of at least a minimum number of payment modes (cash/ credit card/ debit card) at the agent's outlet.
Connect to an Existing OU.


What Documents are required?

Business address authorization letter and license/authorization to carry on to business.

Compliance of KYC (Generally accepted proof of identity and address as required for opening bank accounts as per extant instructions. Illustratively, for proof of identity: Govt. Issued Photo ID or Aadhaar number or PAN card; for proof of address: current or permanent residential address proof or Aadhaar card or current address recorded in bank account.)

Credibility check and credentials verification, account with any bank.
