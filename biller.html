Who is a Biller?

Billers are the service providers who collect payments from customers and participate in the BBPS through BBPOU.
The category of billers participating in BBPS will be specified from time to time.


Types Of Billers
Online-Mode


When Biller is connected online to respective BBPOU and all the communications between Biller and BBPOU is happening on real-time basis. At the time of fetching the bill, BBPOU will pass the required information to the biller and get the response on real time. BBPOU will confirm the bill payment only after getting confirmation from respective biller.

Offline-Mode(A)


When the biller is not connected to the BBPOU on real time basis and provides a file of expected bills on regular basis to respective OU. BBPOU will confirm the bill payment on the basis of the bill information dump provided by the respective biller.

Offline-Mode(B)


When the biller is neither connected to the BBPOU on real time basis nor provides any file. In this scenario, the BBPOU receives all the bill payment requests for that particular biller without any validation.

Benefits To The Biller

As a BBPS Biller, Enjoy Easier Integration


Lower Capex and Opex due to reduced investments.

Wider reach through different channels.
Improved liquidity position due to faster payment collection.

No need to integrate with multiple BBPOUs.
Low entry barriers for small billers or ones confined to small geographies.

BBPS guarantees payment information and multiple settlement of payment transactions at designated intervals through authorized entities. This ensures that there is no separate need for bank guarantees.
Ensure a responsive grievance redressal system (CMS and DMS) that will make the system more responsive and gradually error free.

Sufficient business and technical MIS that may help in analyzing consumer behavior and system performance of biller partners.

Roles & Responsiblities

Biller has to ensure and perform the following:

Agreement with Biller OU

Ensure that an agreement is put in place with the Biller OU appointing it as a default operating unit.

Secure channel for communication

Ensure that the communication between the BBPOU and Biller happens over a secure channel.

Update consumer account

Online Billers must update the consumers account on a real time basis. On receiving receipt of payment, success message should be received from Biller OU.

Share bill information

For offline, it is suggested that the bill information is shared with the Biller OU at regular intervals.

Send alerts

For Billers where bills are paid without fetch, there may be cases where the payments cannot be accounted for any refund requests originating from the customer BBPOU must be promptly responded to.

Reconcile the funds

Billers must reconcile the funds remitted by the Biller OU on a daily basis.

Dispute management

Billers must work with Biller OU to resolve the complaints and disputes within the prescribed TATs.

Handle refund cases

All eligible refund cases must be processed immediately.

Standards

In the event of the Biller OU or the Biller withdrawing from the BBPS system, the biller must continue to offer the bill payment services in a specified period as prescribed from time to time. Biller must close the outstanding complaints, disputes and process all eligible refunds before withdrawal from the system.

Agreement with Biller OU

The Biller should also designate and conclude agreements with back up Biller OU for the sake of business continuity and to ensure continued operations in case the default biller operating unit fails to perform or needs to be changed for some reason.

Agreement with Biller OU

The biller should also insist upon an agreement that a Non-Bank Biller OU will conclude with a sponsor bank indicating the procedure of settlement, designated account for settlement and the guarantee of settlement by the bank.
