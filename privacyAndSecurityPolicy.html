1. PRIVACY AND SECURITY OF WEBSITE

BBPS is fully committed to maintain and protect the privacy, secrecy, security and integrity of the information received by it from persons accessing its website, its members and their customers in accordance with applicable statutes, rules, regulations and directions issued there under. BBPS adopts and implements best international standards and practices for protection, non-disclosure and privacy of information.


When a person visits BBPS's website, the website administrator uses cookies to monitor and collect statistics about visitor's browser, including information about the time and date when a visitor accessed the site, the pages he visited, the Internet domain and IP address from which he accessed the site and information on the browsing software he/she used to access the site. However, no attempt is made to gather or keep personal details to identify users except, in an unlikely event of an investigation, where a law enforcement agency may exercise a power to inspect activity logs.


BBPS's website enables a visitor to access several other websites on which BBPS has no control. BBPS is not responsible for the content and the privacy practices of such websites. BBPS encourages a visitor to examine each website's privacy statement.


a. Collection of Information

Enquiries on the BBPS website (www.bbps.org.in)


When you make an enquiry about BBPS on the BBPS website, you will provide to us:


⚫ Name

⚫ Contact number

⚫ Email address


The personal information you provide in relation to any enquiry will be collected by BBPS for the purpose of resolving the queries and reaching out to you with appropriate resolution. BBPS may also disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if you request it to do so.


b. Storage of Information

As a corporate body, BBPS maintains the records and information in a safe and secured manner as per its policy and in compliance with the statutory provisions and directions for the period required by it and as prescribed by laws and rules etc. BBPS does not allow any unauthorized access to the information stored by it in paper or electronic form. The information is securely stored and access is restricted to authorised personnel only. BBPS incorporates confidentiality clause in non-disclosure agreement with entities having business with BBPS to keep personal information secure and confidential and not to disclose the personal information to others, unless required by law or by an order of a court or by written instruction by BBPS. Such non-disclosure agreements stipulate that all personal information obtained by other party from the arrangement with BBPS will be returned or destroyed on termination/expiry of the non-disclosure agreement.


c. Protection of Information

It is NPCI's commitment to ensure that there are appropriate operational, physical, electronic, procedural and technical safeguards against any unauthorized access or breach of data security to avoid any loss or damage to the owners of confidential information. Some of the salient features of information security system are as under:


(a) Use of firewalls, encryption and data leakage prevention technologies to protect information;


(b) Audit of all vendors and service providers and execution of non-disclosure agreements before availing their services;


(c) Continuous monitoring of BBPS physical and technical environment for vulnerabilities and potential intrusions and implementation of controls to identify and address any concern related to protection of data.


2. EMAIL

Email correspondence sent to BBPS is treated as record and will be retained as required by law. The name and address details of senders are neither added to a mailing list nor disclosed to third parties without consent of the sender unless required by law. Email messages may be monitored by website support staff of BBPS for system trouble shooting and maintenance purposes.


3. CONTACT

If anybody has any query about privacy and security practices of BBPS, he may send his query by email to bbps@npci.org.in
